Pricing FAQ

How do you determine the exact price within each range?
Pricing depends on scope complexity, timeline requirements, team size, and expected business impact. We provide exact pricing after our initial consultation and scope definition call.
Can projects be broken into smaller phases?
Yes. We often recommend phased approaches for larger transformations, allowing you to see results and validate direction before proceeding with additional phases.
Do you offer ongoing retainer arrangements?
We offer ongoing strategic advisory retainers starting at $7.5K/month for companies who want continued strategic support after project completion.
What if the project scope changes during engagement?
Minor adjustments are included. Significant scope changes are discussed and quoted separately with your approval required before proceeding.
How do you guarantee ROI on the investment?
While we can't guarantee specific financial outcomes, our methodology focuses on measurable business impact. Most clients see positive ROI within 6-12 months through increased revenue, cost savings, or operational efficiency.
