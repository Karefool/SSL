Talent Solutions FAQ

How long does it take to implement a talent optimization system?
Most talent system implementations take 6-8 weeks for initial setup, with full optimization occurring over 3-4 months. We work in phases so you see immediate improvements in hiring quality and team performance.
What's the typical ROI of improved hiring systems?
Companies typically see 3-5x ROI within 12 months through reduced bad hire costs, faster time-to-productivity, and improved team performance. Bad hire cost reductions alone often justify the entire investment.
Do you work with companies of all sizes?
We work best with growing companies (10-200 employees) that need systematic talent processes. Smaller teams benefit from our hiring frameworks, while larger companies leverage our performance and development systems.
How do you measure talent system success?
We track multiple metrics including time-to-hire, candidate quality scores, new hire performance ratings, retention rates, and team productivity measures to ensure continuous improvement.
