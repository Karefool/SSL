Partnership Program FAQ

How long does it take to see results from partnerships?
Most companies see their first partner-sourced leads within 60-90 days of launching the program. Revenue typically follows within 4-6 months as partners get fully activated and trained.
What types of partnerships work best?
The most effective partnerships are complementary service providers (consultants, agencies, systems integrators) and technology partners with overlapping customer bases but non-competing solutions.
How do you measure partnership ROI?
We track multiple metrics including partner-sourced leads, deal size impact, sales cycle reduction, customer acquisition cost, and overall partner-attributed revenue vs program investment.
What if we don't have a dedicated partner manager?
We can help you define the partner manager role and responsibilities. Many successful programs start with part-time dedication (20-30%) from a sales or marketing team member.
