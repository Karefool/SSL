Design & UX FAQ

How long does a complete design project take?
Most design projects take 6-8 weeks from initial research to final delivery. Complex projects with multiple platforms may take 10-12 weeks. We work in phases with regular reviews to ensure alignment throughout the process.
Do you provide ongoing design support after launch?
Yes. We offer 30 days of post-launch optimization included with every project, plus ongoing design retainer options for continuous improvements, A/B testing, and seasonal updates.
How do you measure design success?
We track key metrics including conversion rates, user engagement, bounce rates, task completion rates, and overall business KPIs. Every design decision is validated through data and user feedback.
Can you work with our existing brand guidelines?
Absolutely. We can work within existing brand guidelines or help refine and expand them. We'll ensure all designs maintain brand consistency while optimizing for user experience and business goals.
