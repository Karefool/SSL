AI Implementation FAQ

How long does AI implementation typically take?
Most AI implementations take 8-12 weeks for pilot development and testing, followed by 4-8 weeks for full deployment. Complex enterprise solutions may take 4-6 months for complete implementation.
What's the typical ROI timeline for AI projects?
Well-implemented AI solutions typically show measurable ROI within 90 days of deployment. Full ROI realization usually occurs within 12-18 months, depending on the complexity and scope of implementation.
Do we need a large dataset to get started with AI?
Not necessarily. Many AI solutions can work with smaller, high-quality datasets. We focus on optimizing your existing data and can implement solutions that improve data collection for future AI enhancements.
How do you ensure AI solutions integrate with existing systems?
We conduct thorough technical assessments and design AI solutions with robust API integrations. Our approach prioritizes seamless integration with your current tech stack and minimal disruption to existing workflows.
